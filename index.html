A Harrowing Tale of Cheryl Schaefer: Part Human, Part Program

It was a dark and stormy night... It was blustery and cold.
Cheryl was up..and...thriving.
She was missing her left shoe, and her toes were blue.
She broke into a cold sweat of existentialism.
"Am I even human?", she thought, as she stubbed her
frozen toes on a half-empty bottle of peach schnapps and fell into an infinite loop .
Could this story get any worse?
Turns out...maybe..
...
...
...the Schnapps got left behind :(
